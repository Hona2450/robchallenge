Hi, thanks for visiting my website.

My name is Christian Barahona and I am a freshmen that goes to UC Riverside. I am currently in the pre-business program at UC Riverside that consists of taking all of my pre-requisites and major requirments so that I could apply to the business school. I'm planning on picking a concentration in Business Inforamtion Systems in order to explore the more tech aspects of business especially that in this day and age tech is vastly improving.

My plan after college is to work for a big tech company like Google, Facebook, Apple, Amazon, etc. That's the goal but even if I land at another not too known company or startup company, that would be fine as well. I would eventually build up expereince so that when I actually go work at one of those big companies that I am prepared to work there. If a great idea for a product came to me throughout this process and I had the funding and experience for it then I would pursue it and see where I land with it.

I chose to do this fellowship this summer because I feel that it would give me experience in managing a team of individuals in a company and it will also give me a chance to give back to TXT after everything that it has done for me. TXT led me to believe that I have the potential to do anything as long as I am willing to put in the work for it and I want to give that mindset to other young Hispanic and African American kids who need a change in mindset.
